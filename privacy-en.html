Mest App ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy when using the Mest application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We use the collected information to:

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following cases:

To protect your information:
