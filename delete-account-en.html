Follow the steps below to delete your Mest App account and all associated data.

Account Deletion Steps

1 Open the Mest app

Open the Mest application on your phone and log in to your account.

2 Go to Settings

Tap the profile icon in the bottom right corner, then tap the "Settings" button.

3 Find Delete Account option

At the bottom of the Settings page, under "Account" section, tap "Delete Account".

4 Confirm deletion

In the confirmation dialog, tap "Yes, Delete My Account" to complete the process.

Data That Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently deleted within 30 days:

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone, date of birth)
  • Your profile photos
  • Your chat messages
  • Your test results and match history
  • Your Spotify connection information
  • Your notification preferences and settings
  • Your premium membership information

Data That May Be Retained

Due to legal obligations, some data may be retained for specific periods:

  • Payment records (5 years - tax regulations)
  • Security breach reports (for required legal period)

Alternative Method: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, you can send us an email with the following information:

  • Email: info@mestapp.online
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Content: Your registered email address and deletion request

Email requests are processed within 7 business days.
